# Product Context: Antity

## Project Purpose
Antity is a creative exploration of simulated life and organic movement in digital form. It exists as both a visual art project and an experimental platform for simulating basic artificial intelligence behaviors. The project serves as a digital meditation on emergent systems, where complex behaviors arise from simple rules.

## Problems Solved
1. **Digital Art Creation**: Provides a unique, generative visual experience that evolves over time
2. **AI/Life Simulation**: Explores how simple rules can create complex, organic-feeling behaviors
3. **Interactive Experience**: Gives users a way to interact with and influence a digital ecosystem
4. **Educational Visualization**: Demonstrates concepts of emergent behavior and simple AI in an accessible visual format

## How It Works
Antity creates a simulated ecosystem where digital entities ("Antities") move around a canvas, leave behind byproducts, and occasionally reproduce through "fertile" byproducts that can hatch into new entities:

1. **Entity Creation**: Entities spawn at the center of the screen on page load, or where the user clicks
2. **Movement**: Entities move in random directions, changing course periodically with some probability
3. **Byproduct Generation**: As entities move, they randomly generate byproducts (visual trails)
4. **Reproduction**: Some byproducts are marked as "fertile" (eggs) with a small probability
5. **Hatching**: Fertile byproducts incubate and eventually hatch into new entities
6. **Life Cycle**: Entities have a finite lifespan and eventually "die"
7. **Resurrection**: If all entities die and no fertile byproducts remain, a new entity is automatically created

## User Experience Goals
1. **Aesthetic Appeal**: Create a visually interesting and pleasing experience
2. **Emergent Discovery**: Allow users to observe unexpected patterns and behaviors emerging from simple rules
3. **Interactivity**: Provide direct interaction through clicking to spawn new entities
4. **Ambient Experience**: Function as a background visualization that can be both actively engaged with or passively observed
5. **Accessibility**: Work across different devices and screen sizes without complex controls

## Target Audience
1. Digital art enthusiasts
2. People interested in AI and emergent systems
3. Casual observers looking for an ambient, meditative visual experience
4. Educational contexts demonstrating simple AI concepts

## Current Experience State
Antity is currently in an "infant form" where behaviors are primarily based on randomness and probability. The planned evolution is to develop more sophisticated AI behaviors over time, possibly including neural networks or other learning mechanisms that adapt entity behavior based on experience.
